Binary Variables

Bernoulli distribution

\begin{equation} \label{eq:1} Bern(\mathbf{x}|\mu)=\mu^x(1-\mu)^{1-x} \end{equation}

  • \(E[\mathbf{x}]=\mu\)
  • \(var[\mathbf{x}]=\mu(1-\mu)\)

Bernoulli distribution

set \(\mathcal{D}={x_{1},\cdots,x_N}\)

\begin{equation} \label{eq:2} p(\mathcal{D}|\mu)=\prod_{n=1}^Np(x_n|\mu)=\prod_{n=1}^N\mu^{x_n}(1-\mu)^{1-x_{n}} \end{equation}

\begin{equation} \label{eq:3} \ln p(\mathcal{D}|\mu)=\sum_{n=1}^N{x_n\ln \mu+(1-x_n)\ln(1-\mu)} \end{equation}

\begin{equation} \label{eq:4} \mu_{ML}=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{n=1}^Nx_n \end{equation}

Binomial distribution

\begin{equation} \label{eq:5} Bin(m|N,\mu)=C_m^N\mu^m(1-\mu)^{N-m} \end{equation}

  • \(E[m]=N\mu\)
  • \(var[m]=N\mu(1-\mu)\)
